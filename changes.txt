- Pattern matching and destructuring doesn't begin with `.` for tuples, arrays and records `.{...}` becomes `{...}`, or variants `.ok()` becomes `ok()`. `.` is only used in rhs of assignments.
- Interfaces renamed to behaviours
- const renamed to share
- Removed builtin hashmaps and their literals. Records with optional fields provide similar benefits to hashmap literals for providing options to things with added type safety and information
